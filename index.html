About me

I am a recent graduate with a BSc in Land Surveying and Geo-informatics from PolyU. My passion lies in remote sensing and forestry, with a particular focus on lidar and close-range remote sensing technologies. My academic background includes land surveying, geoscience, tree management, and computer science. During my studies, I worked on various projects related to system development, environmental research, data retrieval, and the application of AI in GIS and urban analysis. I am equipped with technical skills in land surveying, GIS, programming, AI/ML development, and proficiency in languages such as R, MATLAB, Java, and Python.
